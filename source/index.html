A scheduling and record keeping app for your children's vaccines and vital statistics.

Schedule

Need to know the details of your child's historic or proposed shots?

Our detailed schedule feature will enable you to look at each shot individually to see exactly how they fit together.

You can also reschedule each shot directly on the app, so you can make sure your children receive their shots how you see fit.



Reminders

Having a hard time keeping track of which shots you're having today and which shots you've rescheduled?

Our Easy Reminder feature will let you know exactly which shots you're having the moment you open the app and let you schedule them as required.



Cloud storage

Worried about losing your smart-phone, your data when you upgrade your phone or that you'll forget to bring your smart-phone to the pediatricians office?

VaccineKeeper™ will store your records in the cloud so you never have to worry about losing themand can access them on any device.



Reports

Need your children's vaccine records for the new school year or a summer camp?

With our Easy Reporting feature you can see your children's shots at a glance and you can email them to yourself or whoever needs them.

You can also access growth charts to see how your children are progressing.



Meet the team

VaccineKeeper is a cloud based software company founded in 2013 by a close team of parents, designers and developers with a passion for building awesome tools for parents.
