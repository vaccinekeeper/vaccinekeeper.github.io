Prospective HIV/AIDS Vaccine

Nov 1st, 2013

After years of trying and numerous failed attempts there finally appears to be some hope for the prevention or curing of HIV.

Early in the year, scientists appear to have cured a toddler born with HIV. In September, scientists located a worthy HIV vaccine candidate. And most recently, scientists succeeded in mapping HIV’s envelope protein , which is a critical step to understanding and modelling how the virus evades our immune system.
